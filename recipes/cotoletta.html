Cotoletta

Description

Cotoletta is a very appetizing italian recipe.

There are a lot of cotoletta's version, but we will talk about the most popular, the "Cotoletta alla Milanese"

Ingredients

  • 4 veal cutlets, bone-in, 3cm thickness
  • 4 eggs
  • 100 g flour
  • 200 g breadcrumbs
  • 300 g clarified butter
  • salt to taste
