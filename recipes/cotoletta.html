Cotoletta

Description

Cotoletta is a very appetizing italian recipe.

There are a lot of cotoletta's version, but we will talk about the most popular, the "Cotoletta alla Milanese"

Ingredients

  • 4 veal cutlets, bone-in, 3cm thickness
  • 4 eggs
  • 100 g flour
  • 200 g breadcrumbs
  • 300 g clarified butter
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. To prepare Milanese cutlets, start by eliminating the visible external fat and connective tissue from the meat. Score it in 2-3 places along the edge to prevent the slices from curling during cooking. Lightly beat the meat. Separately, beat the eggs in a bowl with a fork and dip the slices in, holding them by the bone.
  2. Then pass them in the breadcrumbs, making it adhere well on each side. However, keep the bone very clean, breading it is a mistake. Press lightly with the back of a kitchen knife, creating a sort of grid that will help the breading adhere well to the meat, thus avoiding the formation of air bubbles during frying.
  3. Fry the cutlets in hot clarified butter. Cook the cutlets over high heat for about 2-3 minutes per side. Also sprinkle the bone with a spoon. The cutlets are ready when they are golden. Then place them on a rack or on a sheet of absorbent paper and sprinkle a few flakes of Maldon salt on top.
  4. The Milanese cutlets are ready to be served with salad or baked potatoes.
